Privacy Policy for SiteOps

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SiteOps ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

When you use SiteOps, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your email address, display name, and company name when you create an account.
  • Site and task data: information you enter about construction sites, apartments, rooms, subcontractors, and tasks.
  • Photos: photos you upload to document task completion status.
  • Usage data: basic information about how the app is used to help us improve it.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the SiteOps app and its features.
  • Allow you to collaborate with other members of your construction site team.
  • Maintain records of task progress and completion, including photo evidence.
  • Communicate with you about your account or the app.

Sharing of Information

Information you enter (such as site details, tasks, and photos) is visible to other members of the same site, according to the role and permissions assigned to them within the app. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We use trusted third-party service providers (such as cloud hosting and database providers) to store and process app data securely on our behalf.

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely using industry-standard cloud infrastructure. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Camera and Photo Library Access

SiteOps requests access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to take and upload photos of completed work. These photos are stored securely and associated with the relevant task.

Children's Privacy

SiteOps is intended for use by adults in a professional construction context and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support channel.
